ABOUT HKTR

More Than a Game

Hong Kong Tag Rugby is a community-driven sports organisation built on a simple belief: that tag rugby has the power to transform lives. Founded by Azwar Nusantara, HKTR promotes physical and mental well-being through inclusive, competitive sport — creating opportunities for players of all backgrounds, ages, and abilities.

We are proudly affiliated with the International Tag Federation (ITF) and benchmark our standards against Oztag Australia, ensuring world-class governance and player experience. From grassroots social sessions to elite competitive divisions, we are building a transparent, ethical, and sustainable sports community — and positioning Hong Kong as Asia's undisputed tag rugby powerhouse.

OUR PROGRAMS

Something for Everyone

Men's Open

Competitive open-age division for male players. Fast-paced, strategic, elite-level tag rugby.

Men's 30s

Over-30 division keeping veterans in the game. Experience meets endurance.

Men's Senior

Senior competitive division for seasoned players who still have it.

Women's Open

Premier women's division driving the growth of female tag rugby in Hong Kong.

Mixed Seniors

Co-ed senior division combining experience, tactics, and social connection.

Mixed Opens

Nurturing the next generation of Hong Kong tag rugby talent from the ground up.
